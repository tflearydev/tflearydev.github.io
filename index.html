The world around us is evolving every day. Whether it’s the people, companies, technology, or the environment, changes often present many opportunities and/or many problems. As a kid, I wanted to be an artist (yes, like Picasso), but as I grew older and life presented many challenges, I began having the desire to solve problems and create many solutions for the world as a whole, instead of just on a pallet. This led to an insane amount of studying, self-teaching, and schooling that propelled me to become a software developer, designer, and entrepreneur.

The ability to bring any idea to life is a skill that I take great pride in and strive to improve every second. When I am not studying and implementing technology and business, I enjoy dabbling in real estate and the financial markets. The best thing about it is that all of this can be done while being anywhere in the world!
